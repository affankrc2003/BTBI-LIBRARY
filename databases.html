Research Databases

PubMed

A free resource supporting the search and retrieval of biomedical and life sciences literature.

Access PubMed →

NCBI (National Center for Biotechnology Information)

Provides a suite of databases including GenBank for DNA sequences, and the Protein Data Bank (PDB).

Access NCBI →

EMBL (European Molecular Biology Laboratory)

It is an intergovernmental organization that conducts fundamental research in molecular biology, provides public databases for biological data, and supports cutting-edge life science research globally.

Access EMBL →

DDBJ (DNA Data Bank of Japan)

It is a public database that collects and archives nucleotide sequences from around the world, primarily from Japanese researchers, and makes this data publicly available.

Access DDBJ →

Web of Science

Provides access to multiple databases that reference cross-disciplinary research, which allows for in-depth exploration of specialized sub-fields.

Access Web of Science →
