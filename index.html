About the Department

The Department of Biotechnology & Bioinformatics at Sambalpur University was established to pioneer education and research in the cutting-edge fields of life sciences. It is dedicated to creating a skilled workforce capable of addressing contemporary challenges in healthcare, agriculture, and the environment through modern biological and computational techniques. The department fosters a vibrant academic environment that encourages innovation, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be a center of excellence in Biotechnology and Bioinformatics, recognized for high-quality teaching, impactful research, and producing graduates who are leaders in academia and industry. The mission of the department is to provide comprehensive knowledge, impart advanced practical skills, and cultivate a strong research aptitude among students, preparing them for successful careers and lifelong learning.

Programs Offered

  • M.Sc. in Biotechnology: A two-year postgraduate program focusing on core concepts of biotechnology, including genetic engineering, immunology, and bioprocess technology.
  • M.Sc. in Bioinformatics: A two-year postgraduate program that integrates biology with computer science, covering areas like genomics, proteomics, and computational drug design.
  • Ph.D. Program: The department offers doctoral research programs in various specialized areas of Biotechnology and Bioinformatics, encouraging original research and scholarly contributions.

Key Facilities

The department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and facilities to support advanced research and practical training. These include:

  • Molecular Biology & Genetic Engineering Lab: Equipped with PCR, gel electrophoresis, and other essential tools for molecular studies.
  • Bioinformatics & Computational Biology Lab: High-performance computing clusters with specialized software for sequence analysis, molecular modeling, and data analysis.
  • Cell Culture Laboratory: A sterile facility for animal and plant cell culture experiments.
  • Departmental Library: A dedicated library with a rich collection of textbooks, reference materials, and access to scientific journals, supplementing the central university library.
