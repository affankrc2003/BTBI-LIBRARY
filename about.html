About the Library

Our Mission

The library of the Department of Biotechnology & Bioinformatics at Sambalpur University is dedicated to providing comprehensive resources and services in support of the research, teaching, and learning needs of our students, faculty, and staff. We are committed to fostering an environment of discovery, critical thinking, and scholarly inquiry.

Our Collection

Our collection comprises a wide range of materials, including an extensive selection of books, e-books, and academic journals focused on all aspects of biotechnology and bioinformatics. We also provide access to numerous online databases, digital archives, and specialized software to support cutting-edge research.
